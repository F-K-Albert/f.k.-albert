About Perus Groups

PERUS GROUPS is a dynamic conglomerate founded by F.K. Albert with the goal of becoming the fastest-growing company by connecting economic opportunities between Ghana and Spain.

We leverage strategic sectors and binational networks to facilitate trade, investment, job creation, and sustainable development in both countries.

Our unique structure includes the Basic Needs Association (BNA) focused on eradicating poverty by providing essential services, alongside our investment and insurance arms PIT Funds and PITFIC.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Mission

To empower businesses and communities through scalable, sustainable, and profitable ventures that link the rich resources of Ghana with the advanced markets of Spain.

👁️

Vision

To be the leading business gateway between West Africa and Southern Europe, driving inclusive growth, innovation, and cross-border collaboration.

Our Business Structure

PIT Funds

Our investment division focused on:

  • Raising capital for ventures
  • Providing business systems
  • Blitzscaling businesses
  • Business policy and innovation

PITFIC

Our insurance division focused on:

  • Insurance policies for workers and investors
  • Making Perus a secure company
  • Raising money for investment

Basic Needs Association

Our social impact division focused on:

  • Providing essential services to underserved communities
  • Eradicating poverty through basic needs provision
  • Improving quality of life
